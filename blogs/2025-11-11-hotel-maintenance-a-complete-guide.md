What is hotel maintenance? Hotel maintenance is the ongoing process of inspecting, repairing and servicing a property’s facilities, systems and equipment to ensure everything remains safe, functional and presentable. It extends the life of your hotel assets, reduces costly breakdowns, and ensures a positive guest experience.
