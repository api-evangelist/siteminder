What is a MICE hotel? A MICE hotel is a property designed to host Meetings, Incentives, Conferences, and Exhibitions—the four pillars of corporate event travel. These hotels offer purpose-built spaces and services that business guests need: conference rooms, breakout areas, event technology like AV systems, and on-site accommodation for attendees.
